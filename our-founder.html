A writer, branding specialist, director, businessman & philanthropist… but anyone who has ever worked with him knows he is a visionary dreamer, who trusts his team to take his vision forward with the same unstinting passion, uncompromising creative solutions and undivided attention to each client.

With the legacy of 30 years in brand building, brand management and creating winners across categories regionally, nationally and globally, Shri has earned a great reputation.

The campaigns that he authored built Kalyan Jewellers in to a Rs.10,000+ crore brand and Manapuram Gold Loans to a Rs.6,000 crore brand.

The list of clients Shri has handled include Kalyan Jewellers, Manappuram Finance, Wrangler Jeans, Malayala Manorama, Prime Meridian, Aster, Meriiboy, AVT, Dhanuka Agro Farms, Etisalat, IPL, CCL, Amazon, Etisalat… to name a few.

He has directed more than 700 commercials in 10 Indian languages besides one of the highest-grossing Malayalam films of all time

He has directed ad films with some of the biggest celebrities in India including Amitabh Bachchan, Sachin Tendulkar, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Sushmita Sen, Venkatesh, Mithun Chakraborthy, Mohanlal, Mammootty, Prabhu, Nagarjuna, Shivarajkumar etc

Shri’s foundation takes care of 400 plus Dalit families by providing them homes, toilets, medical care and education

For his winning case studies which turned many clients into a success story, he was awarded a Doctorate in Branding and Communications by The American University, Washington, D.C
